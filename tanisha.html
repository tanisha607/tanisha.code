ABOUT US


NavGurukul Himachal Campus is dedicated to providing quality education to BCA students through a self-study based approach. Our campus, in collaboration with Eternal University, aims to create a conducive learning environment for students. NavGurukul is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on providing affordable and high-quality education in the field of software engineering to underprivileged youth. NavGurukul aims to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing intensive training programs that equip students with the necessary skills to secure jobs in the tech industry. The organization believes in the transformative power of education to uplift individuals and communities. NavGurukul offers a one-year residential program where students, primarily from underserved backgrounds, receive comprehensive training in software development. The curriculum is designed to be rigorous and industry-relevant, covering topics such as coding, data structures, algorithms, web development, and more. A distinctive feature of NavGurukul's education model is its emphasis on self-learning and peer-to-peer education. Students are encouraged to take ownership of their learning journey, collaborate with peers, and develop problem-solving skills. This approach fosters independence and a deeper understanding of the material.

Our Partners

We are proud to be partnered with Eternal University to bring quality education to our students. Educational Collaboration: Eternal University collaborates with NavGurukul to provide quality education in the field of computer science and software engineering. This partnership aims to leverage Eternal University's academic resources and NavGurukul's practical training approach. Degree Programs: The partnership facilitates degree programs for students, particularly in the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) course, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience. Self-Study Approach: Both institutions emphasize a self-study-based approach, encouraging students to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills independently. Benefits for Students: Accredited Degrees: Students can earn accredited degrees from Eternal University while benefiting from NavGurukul's industry-relevant training. Holistic Development: The collaboration provides a holistic education model, combining academic rigor with practical skills and personal development. Career Opportunities: Enhanced career prospects through a blend of academic credentials and practical training, making students job-ready for the tech industry.

One notable partnership is with NavGurukul, focusing on providing quality education and training in software engineering to underprivileged youth. This collaboration aims to bridge the gap between education and employment, particularly in the tech industry. Eternal University stands out for its commitment to quality education, research, and community service. Its holistic approach ensures that students not only excel academically but also develop as responsible and ethical individuals.
